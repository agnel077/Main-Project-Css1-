About BMW

BMW, Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a renowned German multinational company that specializes in manufacturing luxury vehicles, motorcycles,
and engines. Founded in 1916 as an aircraft engine manufacturer, BMW shifted its focus to automobile production after World War I. Over the decades,
BMW has become synonymous with high-quality, high-performance vehicles and has established itself as a prestigious brand in the automotive industry.
BMW's logo, depicting a rotating propeller from its aviation history, symbolizes the company's heritage and commitment to engineering excellence.
Overall, BMW stands as a symbol of luxury, innovation, and driving pleasure, appealing to car enthusiasts and luxury vehicle buyers around the globe.
